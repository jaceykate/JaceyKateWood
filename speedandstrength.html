Only the Disciplined ones in life are free. If you are undisciplined, you are a slave to your moods and passions.

- Eliud Kipchoge.

Having a running and strength coach can make a world of difference for young athletes in any sport. I can attest to this firsthand, I started private lesson with a track coach because I purely wanted to compete in track but after only a few short weeks I noticed a huge improvement in my soccer ability as well. Even after I decided soccer would be my main sport I contiuned with my track lessons learning the value of good speed and conditioning training. Additionally, dedicating time to strength training, a routine I maintain four times a week as a Division One athlete, has been crucial for my development. By starting early with specialized coaching, kids can build the endurance, agility, and strength needed to excel in their chosen sport. Investing in expert guidance from the get-go lays the groundwork for future success on the field or track.
